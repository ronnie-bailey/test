# About Cyber Policy Insights

Founded by Ronnie Bailey, Cyber Policy Insights provides in-depth analysis of cybersecurity regulations, compliance frameworks, and policy developments.

## Our Mission

To demystify cybersecurity governance through:
- Regulatory analysis
- Framework comparisons
- Policy impact assessments
- Compliance best practices

## Expertise Areas
- GDPR & CCPA compliance
- NIST frameworks
- Zero Trust architectures
- Cloud security governance
